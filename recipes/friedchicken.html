Fried Chicken Tendies

Description

Some Smackin tendies so good they'll get you laid or your money back

  • 1 large chicken
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • seasoning of your choice
  • 1 jar of spicy barbecue

Steps

  1. Preheat a skillet with oil of your choosing

  2. Mix the seasonings and batter your chicken to get ready for a deep fry

  3. Once Golden brown, remove chicken from skillet and let cool before dunking into barbecue sauce
